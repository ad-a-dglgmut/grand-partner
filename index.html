ABOUT US

Our Story


The company was founded in 2008 by Kostov Gjorgi and Kostov Mihajlo.

Over the years, "Grand Partner As" has grown into one of the largest companies in the territory of North Macedonia that provides: logistics, transportation and storage of frozen pork, chicken and beef, as well as frozen fish, cheese, and other dairy products.

Our Successes


During 2013, it acquired the prestigious "HACCP Certificate" for quality. During the year, we received permission, the only one in the south-eastern part of Macedonia, to import frozen meat, and all this is the result of dedication to work, belief in quality and excellent attitude towards consumers.
